About us

We are a cozy and welcoming coffee shop located in Black Diamond Washington. We believe that coffee is more than a caffeniated beverage - it's a calling for us to do good in the world. Mercedes Coffee is on a mission to provide a community gathering place - we invite you to come together with your neighbors and friends over a cup of deliciousness.
We firmly believe that our impact on the world should be not only sustainable, but beneficial. Which is why we proudly serve Fair Trade certified coffee.

Our Menu

Unlike other "coffee shops" you may have seen pop up, Mercedes Coffee doesn't believe in mixing a bunch of sugary powders together and claiming it's a healthy beverage. Instead, we offer real drinks made from real ingrediants - fresh roasted coffee, milks from all sources, and teas made from fine leaves. This doesn't mean we don't have fun drinks - we stock a variety of delicious flavored syrups and real whipped cream to spruce up your lattes! Or check out our locally sourced pastry selection, which varies by the day.
